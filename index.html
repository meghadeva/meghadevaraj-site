prompt shelf

My favorite prompts.

A few prompt patterns I come back to when I’m shaping products from fuzzy annoyance into something specific enough to build.

↳

Turn friction into a product brief

“Here is an annoying recurring moment in my life. Ask me clarifying questions, then turn it into a tiny product spec.”

♡

Design for the emotional job

“What is the user trying to feel less of? What are they trying to feel more of? Design the first screen around that.”

✦

Make the next version obvious

“Review this prototype like a sharp product friend. What is confusing, what is promising, and what should I improve next?”
